The Adventures of
Gussie the Bus

After the Covid-19 pandemic rocked our plans for 2020 we sat on our hands waiting for normalcy for nearly a year. We had already owned Gussie and used her as a camper on our 5 acres of land. We were getting restless and decided that getting Gussie roadworthy and comfortable to travel in was priority number 1! We spent the last few months of 2020 and into 2021 doing just that! What you see here is a snippet of our journey across the continental United States.
